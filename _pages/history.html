--- layout: default title: History permalink: history ---
  • Founded in 1967 as a non-profit corporation (#ES 6325) with mental health focus
  • Began as Paquette House, a 12 bed co-ed community based residential facility (24/7 staffing)
  • 1982 name changed to Robert E. Berry House in memory of Robert Berry, Fond du Lac Department of Community Programs Mental Health Director who died in an accident.
  • 1995 downsized to 8 beds
  • 2000 - Established Ahern House - named for Patricia Ahern, long-time member of BH Board of Directors; located 100-102 Marquette St.; supported apartment concept - duplex with 3 bedrooms on each side; co-ed; preferably was a prior resident of Berry House
  • April 19, 2013 - Purchased 2 homes on 1 city parcel at 193 & 195 7th Street
  • May 1, 2013 first resident admitted to 195 7th St; 193 7th St. tenancy began Jan 2014; Each have capacity to house 4.
  • January 9, 2014 7th Street Houses named Heller Houses in honor of Melvin Heller, long-time member of BH Board of Directors
