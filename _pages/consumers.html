Community Based Residential Facility

Berry House is an 8 bed co-ed facility for people living with mental illness, providing supportive living arrangements in a homelike atmosphere, 24 hour staff and weekly RN visits. Referrals to the Berry House program are accepted through mental health agencies. See pictures of our facilities.

While living at Berry House, residents can take part in an Illness Management and Recovery Group or Wellness Recovery Planning, or use the resources of the Fond du Lac Library, YMCA, Friendship Corner, Senior Center and community events.

Program Goals

  • Learning about mental illness
  • Creating a plan to manage symptoms
  • Integrating back into the community

After the Berry House, residents can transition to:

  • Ahern and Heller Houses
    (Supportive living apartments)
  • The Community
  • Their own apartment
  • Home

Requirements

To participate in the Berry House program, individuals must be:

  • Diagnosed with a mental illness
  • 18 years or older
  • Able to exit building independently within two minutes
  • Free from communicable disease
